About

I am currently pursuing a B.S. in Computer Science at Case Western Reserve University, where I am working with Professor Soumya Ray. My broad interests include reinforcement learning, representation learning, and adversarial robustness.

Recent Work

Hierarchical Reinforcement Learning

During the summer of 2022, I worked as a research intern at the Johns Hopkins University Applied Physics Laboratory, where I focused on meta-reinforcement learning. Specifically, I worked on designing and implementing a method of learning composable primitive policies end-to-end. The project is still under active development, with publication planned sometime in the next year.

Active Logic

I previously worked in a research laboratory at the University of Maryland Institute for Advanced Computer Studies studying artificial intelligence and metacognitive systems under the mentorship of Dr. Donald Perlis.
